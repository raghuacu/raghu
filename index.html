Profile

Mr.Raghukumar BS Working as assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at AIT Chikkamaglur. Acquired his Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Vishweswaraya Technical University, Belgaum in 2011. and obtained Master’s degree in Digital Electronics and Communication Systems from Vishweswaraya Technical University, Belgaum in 2014. Pursuing Phd on Medical image processing domain from Adichunchnagiri University BG Nagara. I have 7 years of teaching experience. Interested to work in Digital image processing, BIG Data, Artificial intelligence, internet of things.
